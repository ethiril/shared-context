## Feature

Send a welcome email asynchronously when a user signs up.

## In scope (v1)

- `POST /signup` enqueues a `welcome-email` job carrying `{ userId, email, locale }`.
- Worker consumes the job and sends a single email through the existing SES integration.
- Fire-and-forget semantics: API doesn't wait for or learn about send outcome.

## Out of scope

- Retry policy (deferred to v2).
- Multi-locale templating (English only for v1).
- Click / open tracking.

## Repos

- `api` — owns `POST /signup` and the enqueue.
- `worker` — owns the consumer and the SES send.

## Success criteria

- Successful signup enqueues exactly one job.
- Worker sends exactly one email per job under nominal conditions.
- A worker outage cannot block signup latency.
